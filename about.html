SUMA HAIR CUTTING SALON

OUR GOAL

The goal of Suma Hair Cutting Salon, a 23-year-old Berber-inspired hair salon, is to provide exceptional grooming and hairstyling services while honoring the timeless traditions of the Berber (Amazigh) culture. With over two decades of experience, the salon has built a reputation for blending modern techniques with authentic Berber aesthetics, creating a unique and welcoming atmosphere. Suma Hair Cutting Salon aims to offer personalized care, using natural and eco-friendly products, ensuring customer satisfaction and well-being. By celebrating Berber heritage through its decor, music, and services, the salon strives to be a cultural landmark where clients experience the perfect harmony of tradition and style.

ALEX SMITH

ALEX SMITH the owner of Suma Hair Cutting Salon, is a passionate and dedicated entrepreneur with over 23 years of experience in the grooming industry. Known for his commitment to excellence and cultural appreciation, Alex has built the salon into a beloved establishment that blends modern hairstyling with the rich traditions of Berber (Amazigh) heritage. He named this shop as his younger daughter named "Suma". His vision for Suma Hair Cutting Salon reflects his deep respect for craftsmanship, sustainability, and personalized customer care. Alex’s leadership and love for his craft have made the salon a cultural hub, where clients not only receive top-notch services but also experience the beauty of Berber-inspired artistry.

ROBERT CLANE

Robert Clane, the son of Alex, is the dynamic force behind Suma Hair Cutting Salon in his father's absence. With a deep understanding of the salon's values and a passion for upholding its legacy, Robert ensures the seamless operation of the business. He combines his father's dedication to quality and cultural authenticity with his own modern vision, keeping the salon a thriving space for exceptional grooming services. Robert’s commitment to maintaining the salon’s unique blend of Berber-inspired traditions and contemporary styling reflects his respect for his family’s heritage and his drive to innovate for the future.
